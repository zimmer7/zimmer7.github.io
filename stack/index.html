Our Stack

While this is an ever changing subject we simply select some core technologies we use and love.

Most of modern web projects span a broad range of different technologies. It’s important to choose the right ones, to get up and running quickly and ensure productivity over time.

As a development agency we’ve several years of experience across the whole stack:

  • Server technologies
  • Backend Frameworks
  • Frontend Frameworks
  • Infrastructure Tools

Scroll down to read more about our core technologies.

TYPO3 CMS, Flow and Neos

One of the most popular open source CMS in Europe we work with since 2007.

During all these years we’ve developed a whole slew of different Applications. Starting from simple small low-traffic Microsites to high-traffic Websites including e.g. multilingual content management workflows and webservices.

We attend the TYPO3 UserGroup Rhein-Neckar on a regular basis, sometimes giving talks on various topics.

node.js

node.js, being one of the most rapidly growing server side technologies, caught our eye quite early on.

Since then we have built smaller sites and webservices with it and we like it a lot. It enables a test-driven workflow with a fast pace to get a project up and running quickly, while scaling great.

And because node.js is just JavaScript, you can share code between the backend and the frontend, reducing the time needed for completion.

In 2012 we started the node.js MeetUp Mannheim, meeting every month to share knowledge, ideas and insights.

Chef

We love Chef to tackle modern infrastructure challenges. We use it to manage and automate either our own and our customers IT infrastructure. No matter what operating system or hardware you use, Chef enables a highly flexible and efficient way to manage IT infrastructure.

An automated infrastructure can accelerate your time to market, help you manage scale and complexity, and safeguard your systems. Chef Website - Why Chef?

It makes setting up servers and services documented, repeatable and even testable—what’s not to like?

Apps

Depending on your requirements we can deliver Webapps for iOS, Andriod and if you like some others of the few infrequent environments.

In general we love to build Apps using HTML5, Backbone.js, Foundation and Apache Cordova.
