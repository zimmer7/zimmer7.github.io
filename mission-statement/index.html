Mission

Some People Feel the Rain. Others Just Get Wet Roger Miller

To be outstanding in your field of work, you have to immerse yourself, you have to have passion, you have to feel the rain!

People naturally like doing what they’re good at and are good at doing the things they like. Dave Zwieback

While we certainly think larger agencies can be full service, smaller ones cannot. We’re following this principle in that we only offer services we’re good at, e.g. delegating UX work to specialists in that field from within our ever-growing network. This results in solid solutions:

If you put the right people in the right room they’ll solve the problem the right way. Jeff Hackert

This is our mission and we stick close to it!

Be the change you want to see in the world. Mohandas Gandhi

Quality

The bitterness of poor quality remains long after the sweetness of low price is forgotten. Benjamin Franklin, Aldo Gucci & others…

We firmly believe that quality is one of the major missing part in modern web development projects today.

Quality can be described by many different values, such as

  • good test coverage
  • common coding styles
  • known best practices in structure

With years of experience in our stack, we know we can deliver better quality in time.

Craftsmanship

Craftsmanship names an enduring, basic human impulse, the desire to do a job well for its own sake. Richard Sennet, The Craftsman

We really love our work and so we do care about the two main parts of craftsmanship—knowledge and work.

We’ll keep gaining knowledge of technologies, principles, patterns and heuristics and we’ll keep leaving our comforts zones for the simple desire to do a job well.

Automation

If you do your work by hand,
computers gather at night and laugh about you. unknown

Nobody wants that—right? Besides this point, there are others:

  • you can forget something
  • you accidentally mix up the order
  • it’s not documented

This is why we thrive to automate all things possible, so we can e.g. deploy a project with a single simple command or it’s done entirely automated on every commit into our VCS.

Depending on the project we use everything from Makefiles to grunt, gulp, rake, Jenkins CI and plain old shell-scripts to simplify repeating tasks.

Assurance

High quality is worth nothing if it degrades over time.

To fight that, we employ well known best practices in continous integration and code metrics to ensure and visualize our quality level stays the same or gets better with every commit we make.

Training new coders to our code is easy, because e.g. differences in style, unused methods and variables are pointed out to them and therefore easily fixed.

Agile

While “agile” is definitely one of the most thrown around buzzwords in the last few years, the except from the Agile Manifesto sums up our philosophy pretty nicely:

Responding to change over following a plan Agile Manifesto

When developing a project for you, we want to understand your problem and then iterate on that together with you, to ensure the result is both what you expected and actually solving your problem.

Computers are good at following instructions, but not at reading your mind. Donald Knuth

And so are development agencies…
