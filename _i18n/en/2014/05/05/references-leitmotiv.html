Leitmotiv had the vision to launch a small website with a shop that allows the interested biker to configure their individual high-end bike. Of course, while you configure the bike, both price and weight are updated live, so you always know how much you have to carry both uphill and to the shop.

As they specialize in high-end bikes, they left the decision of server, architecture and framework completely to us. The result is a server managed by Chef, running a lightweight page via node.js, using data and images from Apache CouchDB.
