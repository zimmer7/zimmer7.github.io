If you do your work by hand,
computers gather at night and laugh about you. unknown

Nobody wants that—right? Besides this point, there are others:

  • you can forget something
  • you accidentally mix up the order
  • it’s not documented

This is why we thrive to automate all things possible, so we can e.g. deploy a project with a single simple command or it’s done entirely automated on every commit into our VCS.

Depending on the project we use everything from Makefiles to grunt, gulp, rake, Jenkins CI and plain old shell-scripts to simplify repeating tasks.
